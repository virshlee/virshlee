# Hi, I'm Adam Torok

Physicist, electrical engineer, builder and full-stack developer.

## About

I studied Physics (Nanotechnology) and Electrical Engineering at Technical University of Budapest, then worked on quantum hardware and nuclear simulation before moving deeper into software and product building. I was Founder in Residence at Station F (Pioneers), where I validated startup ideas and built MVPs.

## Featured Project: Samiori

[Samiori](https://www.samiori.com/) is a gamified, AI-powered logistics platform built to replace spreadsheet-heavy operations with a zero-training workflow.

- Video game-like UX for warehouse and fleet teams
- Real-time fleet visibility and operations control
- 3D cargo and warehouse planning
- AI optimization for cargo, routes, staffing, and operations
